Basics

This curriculum is designed to encourage young art students to pursue and learn the fundamentals of drawing in a structured routine, providing plenty of time to sharpen there skills. The curriculum start of with basics like lines and shapes, then build up basic composition skills and continues with intermediate lessons like perspective. This process of slow and deliberate practice allows the student to build observation skills and muscle memory.
